FAQ

What is maph4ck?

maph4ck is an online social networking service where users can trade games with each other in person. After signing in with a Google Account, users can add trading locations, add games to their inventory/playlist, add other users as friends, exchange messages, and create and search trades.

What differentiates maph4ck from other game trading sites?

maph4ck allows users to view the relative locations of the trades they're looking for in a map view in order to make the most convenient decision. This can be done by clicking the MAPHACK! button at the top of the search results page.

What are my locations?

Locations are places where you want to trade games with other users. Your locations are shared with other users so they know if it is convenient to trade with you.

What is my inventory/playlist?

Your inventory keeps track of games that you have and want to trade away; your playlist keeps track of games that you want to trade for. By adding games to your inventory/playlist, you can create several trades without having to re-enter information e.g. game condition for each trade. Also, other users can make you trade offers after looking at your personal profile.

What is a trade?

When you create a trade, you have to add games from your inventory/playlist and/or a cash amount that you want to trade away/for, and an optional trade description. Other users can search for trades, comment on your trade, and view your profile page.

What is my profile?

Your profile is public and contains information about your locations, inventory/playlist and trades. Your contact information can only be viewed by other maph4ck users.

Why should I provide my contact information?

Providing your contact information allows other users to communicate with you directly, when accepting/conducting trades.
